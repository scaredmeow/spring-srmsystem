What happens if the requested documents are not claimed by the time it was scheduled?

Given that the registrar will set a day for the students to pick-up the documents, transcripts not claimed within a year from its due date shall be discarded and its application shall be deemed null and void.

What happens if I am missing a requirement or two?

In an event of insufficient document requirements shown by the student, the registrar would give a remark on to which document is missing and are required to be pass by the student in order for the transaction to take place. Students are given 1 week to comply and not doing so would terminate their transaction.

In case of an emergency, can you let someone other than you to pick up your documents?

Yes, your documents can be claimed by a representative provided that he has an authorization letter from the student and his (representative) valid ID with photo.
